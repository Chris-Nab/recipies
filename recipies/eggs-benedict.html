Eggs Benedict

Eggs Benedict, made easy! Featuring my no-compromise Easy Method for Poached Eggs and a 90-second Foolproof Hollandaise Sauce.

Ingridients:

  • English Muffin
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Hollandaise Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Bring the poaching water to a simmer
  3. Make the hollandaise sauce
  4. Poach the eggs
  5. Toast the English muffins
  6. Assemble the eggs Benedict
