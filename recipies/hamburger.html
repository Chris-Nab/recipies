Hamburger

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingridients:

  • Ground beef
  • Bread rolls
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Oil
  • Vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. Form patties with the ground beef and start cooking it
  2. Toast the bread rolls
  3. Put the Vegetables on the bread rolls
  4. Put the patties on top
