French Fires

Crispy Homemade French Fries

Ingridients:

  • Potatoes
  • Frying oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel Potatoes
  2. Soak in cold water for 30m
  3. Make the hollandaise sauce
  4. Heat Oil in a deep fryer
  5. Fry potatoes in batches
  6. Drain on paper towels and season with salt
